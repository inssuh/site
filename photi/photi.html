Photi

Photi houses the research and the site Photista. Photista is the name of the responsive site that houses marine algae data and the site is built entirely in code with no additional media files (all drawings are coded svgs). The site also contains a poster builder on the homepage that generates random algae facts through a connected json file. The name Photista is a combination of the two words: Photosynthesis and Protista.

Photi also has additional sub projects including a typographic poster, flag design, and a still life rendering in Cinema 4D.
